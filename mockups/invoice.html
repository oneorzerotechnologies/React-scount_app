06C · Create & edit One form, two purposes Same form serves new + edit; from-quote conversion pre-fills everything (terms, remarks, internal). Header swaps to "Edit · INV-478" in edit mode. Due date keeps the green ring as the field most likely to need a tweak.
Notes

Payments are web-only on v1. Mobile shows the payment list and computed status (paid · partially-paid · unpaid · overdue) as read-only. Recording a new payment, voiding one, or marking an invoice paid manually all live on scount.my behind the desktop confirmation flow — high-stakes financial entry shouldn't be a one-tap action on a phone. The Payments block on the detail screen carries a small "Web only" badge to make this explicit.

CRUD on mobile. View = detail screen (06B). Add = create screen (06C). Edit = the same create form, opened pre-filled via the pencil icon in the detail header — header swaps to "Edit · INV-478", Save calls PATCH /v1/invoices/{id}. Delete is in the three-dots overflow behind a confirmation sheet, and is blocked once a payment is recorded on web (must be voided there first).

Additional information. Four fields ride alongside line items: Delivery days, Terms & conditions, Remarks (visible on the customer PDF), and Internal remarks (PDF-hidden, marked with the amber PDF-hidden badge). All four are preserved verbatim across convert-from-quote and round-trip through the edit form.

Status pill colours: DRAFT slate, SENT amber, PAID moss-green, OVERDUE red. PARTIALLY PAID gets the SENT amber treatment with a "Paid RM X of RM Y" sub-line in the detail.

Convert pre-fill. When the user taps "Convert to invoice" on a quote, we open the create form with line items, contact, currency, terms, remarks, and internal remarks pre-filled. The only field that requires explicit confirmation is the due date (defaults to issue + 30 days, highlighted with a green ring).

"Save & send" vs "Save as draft". Primary CTA defaults to "Save & send" — that's the most common path on mobile. "Save as draft" lives in the header overflow.

Share is the only action-bar CTA. Mobile invoices have one primary action: tap Share, OS share sheet opens with share_url from the detail response (a signed link to scount.my/i/<token>). Internal remarks are stripped from the rendered PDF. No Send-reminder button on mobile — chasing overdues is a web-side action; mobile users forward the share link directly.

Tax follows the web. Whether each line item shows a tax pill, whether the totals card has a Tax row, and whether the create form offers a tax-code picker — all of it reads from workspace.settings.tax_enabled on scount.my. If the workspace runs tax-disabled, the app suppresses every tax affordance and tax_minor stays 0. Mobile cannot override the setting.

Billing cycles (recurring). When an invoice is part of a recurring schedule configured on web, the detail view renders an Upcoming cycles card listing the next few invoice dates and amounts — same data as /accounting/invoices/<id> on scount.my. Frequency, anchor day, and end-date are all set on web; the mobile card is read-only and carries a "Web only" badge so users know where to change cadence. One-off invoices simply omit the card.
