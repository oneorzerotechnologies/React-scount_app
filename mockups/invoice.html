Notes

Status pill colours: DRAFT slate, SENT amber, PAID moss-green, OVERDUE red. PARTIALLY PAID gets the SENT amber treatment with a "Paid RM X of RM Y" sub-line in the detail.

Convert pre-fill. When the user taps "Convert to invoice" on a quote, we open the create form with line items, contact, currency, and notes pre-filled. The only field that requires explicit confirmation is the due date (defaults to issue + 30 days, highlighted with a green ring).

"Save & send" vs "Save as draft". Primary CTA defaults to "Save & send" — that's the most common path on mobile. "Save as draft" lives in the header overflow.
